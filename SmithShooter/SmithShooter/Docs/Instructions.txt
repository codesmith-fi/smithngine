Short instructions for SmithShooter:

Menu:
Use menu selection to select either play or quit. Options is not implemented yet.

Play:
Press PgUp/PgDown to zoom in/out
Press BackSpace to immediately stop the ship (debug)
Press End to quit to the main menu
Keyboard:
	Use cursor keys to move
	Use space to shoot
Gamepad 
	Left analog stick to move
	Right trigger to shoot
	
Enemies do not explode yet, they do not shoot. But when you collide with them or shoot them they will start to chase you.

It's a WIP and an example.
